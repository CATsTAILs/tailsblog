## t-SNE
> t-Disributed Stochastic Neighbor Embedding

__Barnes-Hut approximations__ : $n$ 차원에서의 클러스터링 방법 중 하나, 전체 공간을 cube의 형태로 쪼개어 가며 데이터가 없는 부분을 제거한다. 검색하면 나오는 그림이 설명을 다 함.(octatree)

1 Calculate similarity probability $p_{j|i}$ which indicates the similarity of $x_j$ and $x_i$. The point is :$x_i$ would pick $x_j$ as its neighbor ?!

2 Define $p_{ij} = \frac{p_{i|j} + p_{j|i}}{2N}$

3 Define $q_{ij} = \frac{(1 + ||y_i - y_j||)^{-1}}{\sum_k \sum_{l!=k} (1 + ||y_l - y_k||)^{-1}}$, which is formulated based by Student t-distribution.

4 Maximaumlikelyhood = KL divergence between $p_{ij} and q_{ij}$.
