[Corks and exotic ribbons in B4](https://arxiv.org/abs/2103.13967)
> Selman Akbulut

The manifold $Q^4$ called _anticork_, obtained from $B^4$ by removing a properly imbedded smooth disk $D \subset B^4$, bounding the knot $K$.. Figure-eigth?

It provides an example of an exotic homotopy $S^1 \times B^3$ relative to its boundary.

[On the Haefliger-Thurston conjecture](https://arxiv.org/abs/2103.13897)
> Gael Meigniez

The classifying space for the framed Haefliger structures of codim. $q$ and class $C^r$ is $(2q-1)$-connected for $1 \leq r \leq \infty$.

[Handle decomposition of compact orientable 4-manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2103.13602)
> Biplab Basak, Manisha Binjola

A particular class of compact connected orientable PL $4$-manifolds with empty or cnt boundary.
